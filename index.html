FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the commission fixed?

You earn recurring monthly 10% of gross revenue across all services for any number of clients your bring in as long as the client is engaged with us.

What happens if the client increases scope?

Your commission increases as the client’s gross revenue increases.

How long do commissions last?

As long as the client remains engaged with us, the commissions will hit your bank account every month.

Is there a minimum client size threshold?

No. Any client size qualifies. Even if you bring in a client who needs lower volume FTE's, you will still get the 10% monthly-recurring commissions

Is this exclusive to a specific territory?

Absolutely not. You may bring in clients from across the nation. We serve healthcare providers across the nation.

Are there joining fees or upfront costs?

No joining fees. Infact, training, marketing collaterals, business cards and other support will be provided at no cost.
